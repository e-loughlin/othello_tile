About Us

After more than 15 years of experience in Calgary and surrounding areas, Othello Tile Ltd. has become a trusted and preferred tile installation company. We serve many of Southern Alberta’s top builders, architects, and homeowners. As an owner-operator with a hands-on approach, you can be sure you're getting the best results possible!

We have the knowledge and expertise to execute projects of all sizes – from backsplashes to bathroom renovations to small commercial contracts. We also provide Calgary's custom homebuilders with dependable rates and timely project completion. Please inquire about contractor pricing.

We back all of our work with a competitive 2-year warranty on labour and support all product manufacturers' warranties. At Othello Tile Ltd., we are committed to a high standard of reliability, professionalism, and customer satisfaction.

Please contact us to set up a free in-home consultation for your next project, however big or small!
